To use the UCLA Logon service, you need to do the following:

Enable JavaScript in your browser.

Find out how to enable JavaScript.

Enable Cookies in your browser.

Find out how to enable Cookies.

Upgrade to a modern browser.

If you have another browser try signing in with it, or find out how to upgrade your browser.

What is considered a modern browser?

If you are using a work machine and are unable to upgrade browsers, please contact your local IT Support for assistance.

For further assistance, please contact IT Services Client Support at sdhelp@it.ucla.edu.
